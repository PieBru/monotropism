The Monotropism Questionnaire (MQ)

A 47-item self-report measure of monotropic attention — the tendency to focus intensely on a small number of interests at any one time. Indicate how much you agree or disagree with each statement. Your score stays in your browser; nothing is sent anywhere.

Not a diagnostic tool. The MQ describes an attention style, not a condition or a diagnosis. A higher score reflects a more monotropic way of paying attention — a difference, not a deficit. If you are seeking an assessment, please consult a qualified professional.
